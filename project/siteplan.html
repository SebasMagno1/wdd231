Site Name

Personal Finance Hub

This name was selected because the website serves as a central location where users can learn personal finance concepts, track expenses, manage budgets, and improve their financial decision-making skills.

Optional Domain: personalfinancehub.org

Site Purpose

The Personal Finance Hub website helps students and beginners understand money management through educational resources and interactive financial tools.

The website will include:

  • Home page with financial education content.
  • Finance Dashboard page displaying income and expenses from a JSON file.
  • Filtering transactions by category.
  • Modal windows for transaction details.
  • LocalStorage for saving user preferences and budget settings.
  • Financial Resources page with budgeting and saving tips.

Scenarios

  • How can I track my monthly expenses and identify where most of my money is going?
  • What budgeting methods can help me save money as a student?
  • How do I organize expenses into categories such as food, transportation, and savings?
  • Where can I find beginner-friendly financial education resources?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#1B5E20) – Used for headings, navigation, buttons, and important highlights.
  • Light Gray (#F5F5F5) – Used for page background and content sections.
  • Gold (#FFC107) – Used for accents, call-to-action buttons, and special notices.

Typography

  • Poppins – Used for headings and titles.
  • Roboto – Used for paragraphs, lists, and general content.
